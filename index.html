My Story

I am Isaac Abrahamson, a seventeen year-old web developer. I have dreamed about creating websites since I was too young to remember. I created my first website when I was around the age of ten by making several html pages in Microsoft publisher and linking them together with hyperlinks. When I was fourteen, I created a game server, and from there went on to do a complete study of C# and OOP. I have been studying front-end web development alongside C# for almost two years off and on, and I have been going through the FreeCodeCamp training program learning the MEAN stack diligently for the past two months.
